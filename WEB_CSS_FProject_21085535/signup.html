About Us

EVN is a non government organisation dedicated to raising public awareness on environmental problems, ranging from pollution to natural disaster. We firmly believe that if the public are aware of the severity of these issues, they will take up arms and join us in saving our planet, our home by practising eco-friendly habits and supporting related campaigns no matter who and where they are. Through our voices and actions, we know that it is possible to revert our fate as a dying species to a flourishing one by protecting and preserving a sustainable environment for our future generations.
